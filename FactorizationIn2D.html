Factorization in 2D

This tool visualizes two complex sequences derived from prime factorizations:

  • $s_n$: Sum over prime factors with multiplicity ($b_k$). $$ n = \prod_{k=1}^{M_n} b_k \implies s_n = \sum_{k=1}^{M_n} \omega^{b_k} $$ where $M_n$ is the total number of prime factors (counting multiplicity).
  • $\dot{s}_n$: Sum over distinct prime factors ($c_k$). $$ n = \prod_{k=1}^{m_n} c_k^{e_k} \implies \dot{s}_n = \sum_{k=1}^{m_n} \omega^{c_k} $$ where $m_n$ is the number of distinct prime factors.

Here, $\omega = e^{2\pi i / T_o}$ rotates based on the prime value. Primes $p$ correspond to single vectors $\omega^p$, so $|s_p| = |\dot{s}_p| = |\omega^p| = 1$.
